🧠 Neuro-Symbolic AI

Complete Tutorial for Students & Practitioners

Welcome to the World of Neuro-Symbolic AI

Neuro-Symbolic AI is the future of artificial intelligence. It combines the power of neural networks (deep learning) with the precision of symbolic reasoning (logic). In this tutorial, you'll learn how to build intelligent systems that are not only smart but also trustworthy, explainable, and safe.

Whether you want to develop healthcare AI systems, legal document analyzers, insurance fraud detectors, or pharmacy recommendation engines—this curriculum will guide you through every step.

📚 Complete Curriculum

✨ Recommended Learning Paths:

  • 📖 Complete Sequential (All 19 Chapters): Start with foundations, build skills, explore applications, and master advanced techniques. Best for comprehensive learning. Estimated time: 40-60 hours.
  • 🎓 Foundations Only (Chapters 1-3): Master the core concepts of Neuro-Symbolic AI and knowledge graphs. Skip implementations and advanced topics. Estimated time: 5-8 hours. Perfect for understanding fundamentals.
  • 🏗️ Hands-on Projects & Applications (Chapters 4-8): Learn by building real-world systems. Build your first project, then apply neuro-symbolic techniques to healthcare, legal, insurance, and pharmacy domains. Estimated time: 12-18 hours. Best for practitioners.
  • ⚡ Advanced Specialist (Chapters 9-19): Jump into trustworthiness, reasoning types, integration methods, and cutting-edge LLM techniques. For experienced ML engineers. Estimated time: 25-35 hours.
  • 🧠 LLM Reasoning Master (Chapters 14-18): Focus exclusively on advanced reasoning: lifting/lowering, Chain-of-Thought, verification, logic-based reasoning, and inference graphs. Estimated time: 10-15 hours. For LLM engineers.

🏗️ Implementation & Hands-on Projects Breakdown (Chapters 4-8):

  • Project Foundations (Chapter 4): Learn to architect and build your first complete neuro-symbolic AI system. Walk through a medical triage system from data pipeline to deployment. Master the tools, structure, and best practices.
  • Domain Applications (Chapters 5-8): Apply your skills to real-world industries. Build healthcare diagnosis support systems, legal contract analyzers, insurance fraud detection engines, and pharmacy drug discovery tools. Each chapter is a complete mini-project.

🚀 Advanced Track Breakdown (Chapters 9-19):

  • Trustworthiness & Safety (Chapters 9-10): Design consequence-aware AI systems. Master System 1/System 2 thinking and rule-based production systems for safe, explainable AI.
  • Reasoning & Logic (Chapters 11-12): Understand 9 types of human reasoning and formal logic programming. Learn Datalog for declarative knowledge representation.
  • Neural-Symbolic Integration (Chapters 13-14): Bridge neural and symbolic approaches. Master graph embeddings (Node2Vec, TransE), lifting (extracting symbols), and lowering (compressing symbols into neural patterns). Implement KG-RAG pipelines.
  • Advanced LLM Reasoning (Chapters 15-18): Enhance large language models with structure. Learn Chain-of-Thought prompting, fact verification, logic-augmented generation, and inference graphs. Based on peer-reviewed research (Wei et al., Dhuliawala et al., Pan et al., Alotaibi et al.).
  • Capstone: Clinical AI (Chapter 19): Integrate all techniques into production medical AI. Transform clinical guidelines into computable logic with safety guarantees and regulatory compliance.
