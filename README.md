# 🧠 Neuro-Symbolic AI Tutorial Series

Welcome to the **Neuro-Symbolic AI Tutorial Series**—a comprehensive, interactive learning resource for understanding the intersection of neural networks and symbolic AI systems.

This tutorial series was developed as AI-generated course content using Claude Code, inspired by UC Berkeley's renowned DATASCI 290: Neuro-Symbolic AI course. Whether you're a curious student, a researcher, or a developer looking to bridge neural and symbolic approaches, this material is designed to guide you through the key concepts, applications, and best practices.

---

## 📚 About This Course

Neuro-Symbolic AI combines the learning capabilities of neural networks with the reasoning power of symbolic systems. This emerging field is crucial for building AI systems that are not only powerful but also interpretable, trustworthy, and capable of logical reasoning.

This tutorial series covers:
- **Foundational concepts** in Neuro-Symbolic AI and Knowledge Graphs
- **Real-world applications** across healthcare, legal, insurance, and clinical domains
- **Advanced techniques** including chain-of-thought reasoning, graph embeddings, and logic-based language models
- **Production considerations** for deploying Neuro-Symbolic systems at scale

---

By completing this tutorial series, you will be able to:

✅ Understand the fundamentals of Neuro-Symbolic AI and why it matters
